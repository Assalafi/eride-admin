# 🎨 How to Customize Your System Branding

## Quick Start Guide for Administrators

---

## Step 1: Access Settings Page

1. Log in to the admin panel
2. Click on **"Settings"** in the sidebar menu
3. You'll see the System Settings page

---

## Step 2: Change System Name

**Current:** eRide Transport Management

**To Change:**
1. Find "System Name" field under "General Settings"
2. Enter your company name (e.g., "ABC Transport Ltd")
3. Scroll down and click **"Save Settings"**

**What Updates:**
- Sidebar logo text
- Login page header
- Browser tab titles
- All page headers
- Copyright footer

---

## Step 3: Upload Your Logo

**Requirements:**
- File format: JPG, PNG, or SVG
- Maximum size: 2MB
- Recommended: Transparent background PNG
- Recommended dimensions: 200px wide x 50px high

**To Upload:**
1. Scroll to "System Logo" card on the right
2. Click **"Choose File"**
3. Select your logo file
4. See instant preview
5. Scroll down and click **"Save Settings"**

**Where Logo Appears:**
- Sidebar navigation (top left)
- Login page (center)
- Print documents
- Email headers

---

## Step 4: Upload Favicon (Browser Icon)

**Requirements:**
- File format: PNG or ICO
- Maximum size: 1MB
- Recommended size: 32x32 or 64x64 pixels
- Square image

**To Upload:**
1. Scroll to "Browser Favicon" card
2. Click **"Choose File"**
3. Select your favicon file
4. See instant preview
5. Scroll down and click **"Save Settings"**

**Where Favicon Appears:**
- Browser tabs
- Bookmarks
- Browser history
- Mobile home screen shortcuts

---

## Step 5: Update Company Information

**Fields to Update:**
- **Company Email:** Your contact email (e.g., info@yourcompany.com)
- **Company Phone:** Your contact phone (e.g., +234 123 456 7890)
- **Company Address:** Your physical address

**To Update:**
1. Find these fields under "General Settings"
2. Enter your information
3. Click **"Save Settings"**

**Where This Info Appears:**
- Contact pages
- Email notifications
- Footer sections
- Invoice documents

---

## Step 6: Set Financial Defaults

**Settings Available:**
- **Daily Remittance Amount:** Amount drivers pay daily (₦)
- **Charging Cost Per Session:** Cost per charging (₦)

**To Update:**
1. Find these fields under "Financial Settings"
2. Enter amounts (numbers only)
3. Click **"Save Settings"**

**What These Control:**
- Default payment amounts
- Automatic calculations
- Financial reports
- Driver dashboard display

---

## Step 7: Toggle System Features

**Available Toggles:**
- **Enable Maintenance:** Turn maintenance module on/off
- **Enable Notifications:** Turn notifications on/off
- **Require Manager Approval:** Require approval for requests

**To Toggle:**
1. Find switches under "System Preferences"
2. Click switch to enable/disable
3. Click **"Save Settings"**

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

### **For Best Logo Results:**
- Use a transparent PNG file
- Keep logo simple and readable
- Test on both light and dark backgrounds
- Maximum width: 200px for best display

### **For Best Favicon Results:**
- Use 64x64 pixels for high quality
- Keep design simple (will display very small)
- Use contrasting colors
- PNG format works best

### **Design Recommendations:**
- Use your company colors
- Keep branding consistent
- Test on mobile devices
- Check all pages after updating

---

## 🔄 See Changes Instantly

**After clicking "Save Settings":**
1. Refresh your browser (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. Check the login page
3. Check the sidebar
4. Check browser tab icon
5. Open different pages to verify

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### **Before Uploading:**
- ✅ Check file size (Logo: 2MB, Favicon: 1MB)
- ✅ Use correct file format
- ✅ Test image quality
- ✅ Backup current logo/favicon first

### **After Uploading:**
- ✅ Old files are automatically deleted
- ✅ Changes apply immediately
- ✅ Cache is cleared automatically
- ✅ All users see changes instantly

### **If Something Goes Wrong:**
- 🔄 Refresh your browser
- 🔄 Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
- 🔄 Re-upload the file
- 🔄 Contact technical support
